Welcome to Xenotaph: Embark on a Cosmic Odyssey

Xenotaph is a lore-rich turn-based strategy game that seamlessly merges classic board game elements, card deck building, and puzzles, offering an enigmatic future as its backdrop. In this cosmic odyssey, players assume the role of Remnants, the last flickering embers of humanity, tasked with resurrecting a fractured civilization from annihilation.
